Welcome to Jer's Great Summer Challenge!

Teams

You have been split into teams based mostly on how I know you, and we will compete all summer long to determine the best team. Team sizes are generally flexible and not everyone needs to play in every event, but the more members that join the higher your team's chances of scoring points will be.

Events

Each event will have it's own rules and entry fee if applicable. They will include everything from online challenges like March Madness brackets to beer pong, trivia nights and more. For team events you should play with a team member whenever possible but it's ok to play cross teams if you need to, your points just won't count in full towards the greater challenge.

Captains

The first two players on each team are designated captains. There aren't any real roles involved, just drive participation in the events for your teams!

Me

I (Jer) will only compete in events where it makes sense, for example if I'm hosting trivia or mystery events I won't be playing in them, but I will for events like beer pong and March Madness.
